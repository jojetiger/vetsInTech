Hikes With Hodu

Saint Edward State Park

This trail is where we took hodu for his very first hike. It is only a 3-mile hike with 400 ft. elevation gain. The best part of this hike is that it leads you down to a gordgeous view of the lake washington! Yes, you would go down hill first for this hike, and then finish it strong by going uphill.

Get Directions to Saint Edward State Park

Fragrance Lake Trail

This trail is part of Larrabee State Park in Bellingham, Washington. It is 5.5 miles roundtrip and there is about 1 mile loop around the lake where you can enjoy the serene views of the lake. The trail is well maintined so it makes really easy to go hike with your little pup!

Get Directions to Fragrance Lake Trailhead

Poo Poo point Trail

Poo Poo Point is 7.2 miles round trip hike with 1748 ft. elevation gain. It you've never hiked with your little pup, I would go on an easier hike first before you try this one. The best part of this hike is that you will have a great view of Mt. Rainier on a clear day, although hodu never had luck to enjoy the view.
